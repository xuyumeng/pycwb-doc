pycwb.modules.job_segment.dq_segment.get_job_list(ifos, dq_list, seg_len, seg_mls, seg_edge)[source]¶

Build the job segment list.

The job segments are builded starting from the input ilist, each segment must have a minimum length of segMLS+2segEdge and a maximum length of segLen+2*segEdge in order to maximize the input live time each segment with lenght<2*(segLen+segEdge) is divided in 2 segments with length<segLen+2*segEdge
